Privacy Policy for Cat Clicker

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Cat Clicker respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use the game.

Data Collection

Cat Clicker does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, photos, contacts, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, browsing history, purchase history, or any other personal information.

Local Game Data

Cat Clicker may save game progress locally on your device, such as your clicks, kibbles, upgrades, unlocked skins, equipped skin, settings, and other gameplay progress.

This data stays on your device. It is not sent to the developer, advertisers, analytics services, or any third-party service by this app.

Analytics and Tracking

Cat Clicker does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, tracking technologies, third-party advertising SDKs, or user profiling services.

The app does not track you across apps or websites.

Internet Access and Accounts

Cat Clicker does not require an account and does not provide online chat, messaging, unrestricted web browsing, or user-generated content sharing.

Children's Privacy

Cat Clicker is designed as a simple casual game. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Third-Party Services

Cat Clicker does not currently use third-party services that collect data from users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app's features change. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer at ProgrammingCodingScripting@gmail.com.
